Representative Workflow


The Representative Workflow is used to model malaria transmission for generic locations.


The Representative Workflow builds a simulation using Representative Data. Either of two models can be used:

  • EMOD {% include "transmission_simulator/more_on_emod.html" %} (The Institute for Disease Modeling), and
  • OpenMalaria {% include "transmission_simulator/more_on_om.html" %} (Swiss Tropical and Public Health Institute).


The Representative Workflow helps build a malaria transmission baseline by making a series of guided choices from among a small range of settings (typically High, Medium, or Low) that end up defining the weather pattern, the demographic characteristics, the vector or vectors and the parasites present and their endemicity.


The resulting baseline may be designed to represent malaria transmission under conditions that closely resemble a true site, but strictly speaking the site produced is hypothetical.


The main goal of this workflow is to provide a relatively simple means of exploring transmission and interventions in a range of different hypothetical locations.
