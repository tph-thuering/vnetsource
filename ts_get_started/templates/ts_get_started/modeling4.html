Prototype 1
Only contains weather file generation. In the future it could contain demographics generation, vector species generation, and transmission intensity selection. Data for weather is randomly generated based on predefined numbers to attempt to form semi-realistic data.
Prototype 2
Contains weather file generation as well the vector species and their feeding behaviours. This component was designed as a calibration tool and not as a representative workflow, but has the potential to satisfy those requirements. Data for weather is completely predefined and thus has less chance of creating weather data that is unrealistic.
